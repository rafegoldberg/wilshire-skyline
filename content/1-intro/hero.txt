Title: Intro

Text: 

Wilshire Skyline manages (link: #property=villa-rebecca text: a portfolio of luxury multi-family residential) properties located in prime sub-markets in Los Angeles. In addition to other real estate holdings, the principals of Wilshire Skyline own a controlling interest in all of the properties under management.

Currently, Wilshire Skyline manages 18 multi-family residential properties (499 units/ 384,689 square feet) located in Malibu, Santa Monica, Beverly Hills, West Hollywood and Hollywood.
